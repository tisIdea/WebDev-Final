Artist of the Month

BEABADOOBEE — May 2025

beabadoobee (Beatrice Laus) is a Filipino-British singer-songwriter who has captivated listeners worldwide with her distinctive blend of bedroom pop, indie rock, and 90s-inspired sounds. Rising to prominence with her introspective lyrics and nostalgic melodies, she has established herself as one of the most compelling voices in contemporary indie music.

Originally gaining attention with her viral hit "Coffee," beabadoobee has evolved into a critically acclaimed artist with a devoted global following. Her latest album showcases her growth as a songwriter, featuring more refined production while maintaining the raw emotional honesty that fans have come to love.

Don't miss: beabadoobee will be performing in Manila this August as part of her 2025 world tour! This homecoming concert marks a special moment as she returns to her Filipino roots.

Concert Preview: Manila Homecoming

Josephus
Music Lover
May 10, 2025

beabadoobee's upcoming Manila concert this August is shaping up to be one of the most anticipated musical events in the Philippines this year. The Filipino-British artist's return to her birthplace represents a full-circle moment in her remarkable career journey.

Set to perform at the Mall of Asia Arena on August 15, this concert marks beabadoobee's most significant Philippine show to date. Fans can expect an emotionally charged performance as she brings her signature blend of 90s-influenced indie rock and intimate bedroom pop to her homeland.

Having achieved international success with her distinctive sound and authentic songwriting, beabadoobee's connection to her Filipino heritage has remained an important part of her identity. In recent interviews, she has expressed excitement about reconnecting with her roots and performing for fans who have supported her since her early days uploading songs online.

The Manila concert is expected to feature tracks spanning her entire discography, from early releases like "Coffee" through her acclaimed albums "Fake It Flowers" and "Beatopia," and including material from her latest work. Her live performances are known for their raw energy and the intimate connection she establishes with her audience.

More than just a concert, this homecoming represents a cultural moment that celebrates Filipino talent on the global stage. For fans in Manila, this August show offers a rare opportunity to experience beabadoobee's music in the country where her journey began, completing a beautiful narrative arc in her still-evolving career.
