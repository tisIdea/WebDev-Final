Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book studio time?

You can book studio time by filling out the contact form above, calling our booking line, or sending us an email with your preferred dates and times. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for evening and weekend sessions.

What equipment do you have available?

Our studio is equipped with top-of-the-line recording equipment, including Neumann microphones, Pro Tools, Logic Pro X, various instruments, and vintage analog gear. For a complete list of our equipment, please contact us directly.

Do you offer mixing and mastering services?

Yes, we offer professional mixing and mastering services by experienced engineers. These services can be booked separately or as part of a recording package. Contact us for rates and availability.

How do I submit my music for consideration?

We're always looking for new talent! You can submit your music by sending us links to your work through our contact form. Please include information about yourself, your musical style, and your goals as an artist.
