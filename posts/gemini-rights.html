Steve Lacy’s “Gemini Rights” and the Rise of Alternative R&B

Posted by Josephus | May 9, 2025
Tags: R&B, Soul, Indie

Steve Lacy’s Gemini Rights is a bold step into the spotlight. Formerly known for his guitar work with The Internet and DIY SoundCloud hits, Lacy crafts a fully realized solo record that’s lush, raw, and wildly original.

The album dances across genres — infusing funk, indie rock, and R&B into a melting pot of stylish grooves and emotional confessions. Tracks like “Bad Habit” became TikTok anthems, but the album’s real charm lies in its authenticity and unpredictability.

Unlike polished pop records, Gemini Rights embraces its imperfections. It’s messy in the best way — like real feelings, real heartbreaks, and real transformations. It proves that bedroom pop can grow up without selling out.
